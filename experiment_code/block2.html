Questionnaire

There is a small stack of papers on the top left corner of your desk. Please turn only the top paper (questionnaire block 1) over and fill in the questionnaire. Once you are finished put the paper face down on the top right corner of your desk. Do not look at or fill in any other questionnaire yet!.
After the questionnaire you can start with block 2. This block you will be playing 20 rounds again but with a different player.
Once you are ready to start with block 2 click on the "Ready Up" button and the block will start as soon as the other player is ready.
Remember, the goal of the game is to get as many points as possible.
